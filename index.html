Welcome to Snake Snack!
Silvers the Snake thank you for coming, he is starting to become quite hungry. However, he isn't the best at finding his food. Will you help Silvers to get the food so he can grow and not keep going hungry?

I am sure you already know this, but lets just go over it one more time for the people in the back!

To control Silvers the Snake on the screen you have to push the arrow keys. Make sure you keep away from hitting the border or yourself it will make you go game over. Make him grow and earn points for as long as you can. Let's see how far you can go!

Food
Every time you eat one of the apples you grow both in length and in points.

About Us

Snake Snack is based on the snake game most people know. Some grew up with it, as one of their first games on there mobile, others learned to love it over time. It is a game that at first dosn't take to much thought and skill, but as the snake grows it gets harder to beat your own high score! Can you beat your own score?

It is a game that easily takes up a few hours if you are not carefull. However, most of us can't seem to stay away for to long. This is where Snake Snack comes in. A project that will grow over time. With graphic design created specially for this website, and more food designs in development.
Want to hear more? Well then go to the and send a message this way, I would love to hear from you.

Snake Snack was created as a project for my second portfolio project for Code Institute Full Stack Development Diploma.

I wanted to create a game that that I already loved, and then could make my own in some ways.
And who could really go wrong with a beloved game like snake?
To me snake is classic game that never dies. It is one of the games I remember the best from playing on my phone when I was younger, and that might be because even though it has been here since the 1970's it still is seen quite often in new games or and older version suddenly appearing. It is defiantly not a game that you forget suddenly.

I choose a game I will be able to add on to because I love the idea behind the project. It isn't just soemthing I have to make but something I am truly exited about creating.
